About GateIN

We are a software company that specializes in security and clearance solutions.

Our software provides a simple and effective answer to the bottleneck caused by the still analogue check-in and check-out procedures in many point of entries.

Our system digitizes the whole process and as a result greatly reduces the time taken. Improving the process for all parties involved.




How-to

The user downloads the app. Being a first-time user, one is required to register and fill in their details, for their profiles to be created, as well as to get their pin.

Using this pin, users can access different places with ease, and the guard can also use the pin to verify the details of the people any day or time.

Residents can also make appointments for their guests, and the guards will be able to tell who and when they will arrive, hence an effective check in and checkout process.



Our Users

GateIN can be used by anyone accessing guarded places.

Security guards use the app to check in and check out the people coming in and out of a particular premise.

It also used by anyone who wants to access the premises where our application is being utilized.

Further, GateIn can be used by residents who would like their guests to have an easy time at the entrance.



Where-to

The app is applicable in several places:

Malls

Schools/Colleges

Office buildings

Apartments/Residentials

Places of Worship (Church, Mosque, Temples etc)

Amongst other guarded premises.
